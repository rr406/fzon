Annie Granny Turning One

Getting ready for Anu's first brithday!

Prelude - Part 1
Arvind trying on Birthday Cap while Anu babbles away, Anu gives appreciative squeal when Arvind finally gets cap on

Prelude - Part 2
Mum gets Anu to try on cap. Anu takes it off, Arv babbling away

Prelude - Part 3
Arvind going trick or treating with birthday cap as basket. Anu demands her share of birthday caps - DAA DAA DAA. Arv unhappy about it. Arvind asks who are you looking at dad ?

Prelude - Part 4
Mum unveils Anu's birthday dress, Anu grabs it and tries to put it on herself without success. Mum says time to take off and put away the birthday caps. Arv wants to know which stuff to take for trick or treating

Prelude - Part 5
Anu calling dad - THAAAAAA Anu tottering around trying to help put things away, picks up Arv's sandals to put away. Anu comes to dad for a small chat, then escapes from in between dad's legs. Arv searching for his stickers

Prelude - Part 6
Mum calls Anu to try the dress on her. Anu putzes around a bit trying to put one candy in the Halloween bucket. Then runs to window, points at sticker and goes AAAAAAH excitedly. Messes up the neatly piled birthday caps on the window sill. Surveys the damage and gives a smile. Then tries unsuccessfully to put caps together again. Arv whining for Halloween sweets

Prelude - Part 7
Mum finally gets to put the dress on for Anu. Tantrum unleashed. Anu points to and kisses balloon. General babbling by both

Prelude - Part 8
Trapped between dad's legs. Escapes with mighty scream. Both putzing around with Halloween sweets. Arv tries one and says it tastes like it is from the Indian store. Mum tries to button up Anu's dress and faces another tantrum. Anu spots vacuum cleaner and makes scary sound. Anu says Bye Bye to Mum
